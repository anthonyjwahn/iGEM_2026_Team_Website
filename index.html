Past Projects and Achievements

  • Oncurex - 2024

    Optimizing the biosynthesis and delivery of ursolic acid in yeast for oncology.

    Achievements
    • Achieved gold medal classification
    • Nominated for Best Presentation
  • ENERGEM - 2023

    E. coli engineered to produce enzymes to synthesize beneficial high-value methylxanthines in a cell-free immobilized-enzyme reactor.

    Achievements
    • Achieved gold medal classification
  • MicroMurals - 2022

    Handheld 3D bioprinter aimed at bridging the gap between the arts and STEM in early interdisciplinary education.

    Achievements
    • Achieved gold medal classification
  • Collatrix - 2021

    Protein-based hydrogel scaffolds for wound healing research and applications and optimal tissue repair.

    Archievements
    • Achieved silver medal classification
  • Lumicure - 2020

    E. coli engineered to produce fluorescent reporter and a cancer therapeutic for metastatic breast cancer.

    Achievements
    • Achieved gold medal classification
  • Rehab - 2019

    Developing a community-oriented Harmful Algal Bloom detection and remediation system.

    Achievements
    • Achieved gold medal classification
  • Oscillate - 2018

    A novel frequency-based biological band-pass filter

    Achievements
    • Achieved bronze medal classification
  • OxyPonics - 2017

    A biologically integrated hardware system that uses oxidative stress to boost hydroponic crop yields

    Achievements
    • Achieved gold medal classification
    • Won Best Supporting Entrepreneurship at the 2017 iGEM Giant Jamboree
  • Legendairy - 2016

    A bacteriocin treatment and customizable preventative milking shell for bovine mastitis

    Achievements
    • Achieved silver medal classification
    • Nominated for Best Integrated Practices at the 2016 iGEM Giant Jamboree
  • fishPHARM - 2015

    Novel drug delivery system and probiotic treatment for Bacterial Coldwater Disease and app for monitoring nutrient content in fish hatcheries

    Achievements
    • Achieved gold medal classification for fifth consecutive year
    • Won Best Supporting Entrepreneurship, Best Environmental Project, and Best Applied Design
  • Lead it Go - 2014

    Continuous water filtration system to sequester heavy metals

    Achievements
    • Achieved gold medal classification for fourth consecutive year
  • Organofoam - 2013

    Comprehensive genetic toolkit for fungal engineering

    Achievements
    • Achieved gold medal classification for third consecutive year
    • Won Best Human Practices Advance at North American iGEM competition
    • Won Best Human Practices Advance at iGEM international competition
  • Safe Bet - 2012

    Real-time arsenic biosensor

    Achievements
    • Awarded top 4 finalist regionally, won Best Wiki and Best Human Practices Advance awards
    • Top 16 finalist worldwide, won 'Best Solution to an Oil Sands Problem' from the Oil Sands Leadership Initiative
