Our team is made from students from every year, every college, and every major. From gene-splicing in wet-lab to pursuing business endeavors to organizing outreach events, bright passionate students make this team thrive. Below is the breakdown of what a year on iGEM looks like:

  • Spring: brainstorm next project and new member training (2 credits, 6-8 hrs/week)
  • Summer: on-campus project work (20+ hrs/week), all new team members (except for business and wiki subteams) are expected to stay in Ithaca over the summer
  • Fall: finish project and compete at iGEM jamboree (3 credits, 12-16 hrs/week)

If you have any questions, feel free to check out our FAQ page or email us cornelligem@gmail.com.
